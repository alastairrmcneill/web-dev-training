Alien One

Discover alien one, the indoor rock climbing destination like no other. Housed in a former church building, our vaulted ceiling is home to handcrafted and truly unique climbing surfaces that will leave you saying "Wow!" From easy slabs to overhanging roofs, we've got something for every climber. With 200 different routes that change every 4 months, you'll never be bored. Plus, we offer top-roping, leading, and bouldering all in one location. Register for free and come experience the thrill of climbing at alien one.

Alien Bloc

At Alien Bloc, our dedicated bouldering centre is perfect for climbers of all ages and abilities. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, visiting alone or with friends, our indoor bouldering facility is designed to provide a fun and accessible climbing experience. With a maximum wall height of 4.7 meters and firm matting throughout, you can enjoy the thrill of climbing without the need for ropes or technical skills. Join us and embark on a rock climbing adventure today!
